Welcome to Amer Maharaja

Empowering Minds in a Royal Setting

Amer Maharaja P.G. College, located near the majestic Aravali hills of Kunda, Amer, is a premier institution dedicated to academic brilliance. Our campus offers a self-contained environment conducive to deep learning and personal growth.

With over 12 specialized courses across 6 degrees, we cater to more than 1,300 students, maintaining a favorable student-teacher ratio to ensure individual attention.
